A rich, velvety "peanut butter and jelly" smoothie packed with antioxidants, healthy fats, and supergreen nutrients. Pre-blended berries, oat milk, and creamy Greek yogurt mask the earthy green powder for a smooth, café-style breakfast. Makes 1 large serving — about 5 minutes of prep plus 1 minute of blending.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity |
|:-:|:-:|
| Vanilla Extract | 1/4 teaspoon |
| Smooth Peanut Butter | 1 tablespoon |
| Greek Yogurt (Plain or Vanilla) | 1/2 cup (120 g) |
| Oat Milk (Liquid Base) | 1 cup (240 mL) |
| Pre-Blended Berry Mix[^2] | 350 mL (approx. 1 1/2 cups) |
| Flaxmeal (Ground Flaxseed) | 1 tablespoon |
| Zena Nutrition Organic Supergreens, Mixed Berry[^1] | 1 stick pack (11.5 g) |

## Instructions

1. **Layer from liquid to solid** to keep dry powder and sticky peanut butter off the blades and lid. Pour the oat milk into the bottom of the blender jar.
2. Add the supergreens powder, flaxmeal, and vanilla extract directly into the liquid.
3. Spoon the Greek yogurt and peanut butter into the center of the mix.
4. Top with the pre-blended berry mix.
5. Secure the lid tightly and blend on high for a full 60 seconds, until the peanut butter emulsifies and the fibrous greens powder dissolves completely.
6. (Optional) If you see flecks of flaxmeal or stubborn berry-seed fragments, blend for another 15-20 seconds to fully liquefy.
7. Pour immediately into a tall glass or mason jar and enjoy fresh!

## Notes

* **Nutritional boost:** The flaxmeal and peanut butter add healthy fats that help your body absorb the fat-soluble vitamins (like Vitamins A and K) in the supergreens powder.
* **Lighter base:** To lower the calorie and sugar profile, swap the oat milk for 1 cup of unsweetened almond milk or coconut water.

[^1]: [Zena Nutrition Organic Supergreens](https://zenanutrition.com/), Mixed Berry flavor — a single-serving stick pack greens powder.
[^2]: Frozen strawberries, blackberries, and raspberries broken down with a little water beforehand.

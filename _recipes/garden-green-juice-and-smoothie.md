A fresh green juice paired with a creamy smoothie made from the leftover pulp — zero waste, two drinks.

## Ingredients

### Juice
| Ingredient | Quantity |
|:-:|:-:|
| Granny Smith Apple | 1 |
| Kale | 2 Stalks |
| Water | 1 Cup |
| Fresh Ginger | 1 Teaspoon |
| Lemon Juice | 1 Tablespoon |

### Smoothie
| Ingredient | Quantity |
|:-:|:-:|
| Green Juice Pulp | 2 Cups |
| Greek Yogurt | 1 Cup |
| Milk | 1/2 Cup |

## Instructions

### Juice
1. Combine apple, kale, water, ginger, and lemon juice in a blender.
2. Blend until smooth.
3. Pour through a fine mesh sieve, pressing to extract as much juice as possible. Reserve the pulp.
4. Serve chilled.

### Smoothie
1. Combine reserved pulp, Greek yogurt, and milk in a blender.
2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
3. Serve immediately.

A simple cheese sauce made from Velveeta. Perfect for savory crepe fillings or as a topping.

*Image sourced from Creative Commons.*

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity |
| :-: | :-: |
| Butter | 2 Tablespoons |
| All-Purpose Flour | 2 Tablespoons |
| Heavy Cream | ¾ Cup |
| Velveeta | 8 Ounces (2 chunks, diced or cubed small) |
| Salt | To Taste |

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a small saucepan over medium heat.
2. Whisk in flour to create a light roux, stirring constantly for about 1 minute.
3. Gradually pour in cream while whisking to avoid lumps.
4. Continue whisking until the mixture thickens slightly, about 2-3 minutes.
5. Add diced Velveeta in small batches, stirring constantly until fully melted and smooth.
6. Season with salt to taste (note: Velveeta is already salty, so taste as you go).
7. Remove from heat and serve warm.

> If sauce becomes too thick, add additional cream beyond the ¾ cup base to reach desired consistency. Original recipe noted "too much roux, too little milk/cream" — err on the side of more cream. Needs more salt than you'd expect; taste and adjust accordingly.

A simple berry compote made by cooking mixed berries down into a chunky syrup. Perfect topping for sweet crepes.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity |
| :-: | :-: |
| Mixed Berries | 1-2 Cups (fresh or frozen) |
| Lemon Juice | 1 Tablespoon |
| Sugar | 2 Tablespoons |

## Instructions

1. Add berries, lemon juice, and sugar to a small pot over medium-high heat.
2. Bring to a simmer, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.
3. Cook for about 8-10 minutes, or until berries break down into a chunky syrup.
4. Remove from heat and let cool slightly before serving.

> Serve the compote warm or at room temperature. It can be made ahead and reheated gently before serving. Use a blend of raspberries, blackberries, and strawberries, or your preferred combination. Fresh berries are preferred, but frozen berries work well since they'll cook down anyway. Original recipe used 1 tablespoon sugar but was found to be too sour. 2 tablespoons balances the tartness of the berries and lemon juice.

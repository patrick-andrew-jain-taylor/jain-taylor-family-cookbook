A grab-and-go breakfast you build the night before — 5 minutes of prep, then the fridge does the rest. Quantities below are for **1 jar**; multiply by however many jars you want. They keep about 5 days refrigerated.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity (per jar) |
|:-:|:-:|
| Rolled Oats | 1/2 cup |
| Milk | 1/3 cup |
| Yogurt | 1/4 cup |
| Peanut Butter | 1 tablespoon |
| Frozen Berries | ~1/3 cup |

## Instructions

1. Add the oats to a jar (pint or quart).
2. Pour in the milk and yogurt; stir until the oats are fully submerged.
3. Swirl in the peanut butter.
4. Top with the frozen berries, leaving ~1/2 inch of headspace below the lid.
5. Lid on, refrigerate overnight. Eat cold; stir before eating if desired.

> Scaling up? Make a row of jars at once — they store about 5 days, so a few jars cover most of the week.

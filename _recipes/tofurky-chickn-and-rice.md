Go out and buy the biggest bottle of Frank's you can -- you're going to need it!

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity |
|:-:|:-:|
| Tofurky Plant-Based Chick'n | 1 Package |
| Jasmine Rice | 1 2 lb bag |
| Red Bell Pepper | 6 |
| Onion | 2 |
| Garlic | 1 Bulb |
| Great Northern Beans | 2 cans |
| Tomato Paste | 1 6 oz (170 g) can |
| Vegetable Broth | 2 boxes |
| Olive Oil | To Taste |
| Salt | To Taste |
| Black Pepper | To Taste |
| Red Crushed Pepper | To Taste |
| Linguica Seasoning[^2] | To Taste |
| Portuguese All Spice[^2] | To Taste |

## Instructions

1. Chop red pepper, onion, and garlic.
2. Sauté red pepper, onion, and garlic in olive oil in a large pot[^1] until vegetables are soft.
3. Add Great Northern Beans with their can liquid, the 2 boxes of vegetable broth, and an equivalent amount of water.
4. Add salt, pepper, red crushed pepper, linguica seasoning, portuguese all spice, and tomato paste.
5. Bring to a boil.
6. Remove about 8 cups of broth from the pot and set aside.
7. Add the full 2 lb bag of Jasmine rice. Turn temp down to low and cover.
8. Stir every 15 minutes, adding reserved broth as needed to prevent sticking.
9. Cook until rice is soft (90 minutes to 2 hours depending on desired firmness)[^3]
10. Let rest before serving to allow the rice to absorb any residual liquid.

> Serve with Frank's Red Hot!

[^1]: A dutch oven is perfect for this recipe.
[^2]: Linguica Seasoning & Portuguese All Spice are optional, but add an extra spicy, sweet, and sour element. If you do use these spices, use sparingly; no more than a tablespoon's worth each.
[^3]: Use the reserved broth to add liquid as needed while the rice cooks.
